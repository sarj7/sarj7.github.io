About me

I am a seasoned professional in the field of Deep Learning and Computer Science, currently pursuing my Master of Engineering at the University of Calgary. With a comprehensive academic background, including Bachelor of Technology in Information Technology from Maharaja Surajmal Institute of Technology, I bring a strong foundation in both theoretical knowledge and practical application.

My professional journey includes a rewarding role as a Research Associate at Jawaharlal Nehru University's Genome Science and Technology Lab. Under the supervision of Prof. Binay Ranjan Panda, I have been actively engaged in utilizing state of the art deep learning approaches for various application. One such task was to predict azadirachtin content in Azadirachta Indica fruits and sweetness in Citrus nobilis x Citrus deliciosa fruits from the respective fruit image. Additionally, my work extends to developing a fall detection system for assisted living applications, showcasing my proficiency in computer vision.

These experiences, coupled with my academic achievements and project involvements, reflect my dedication to leveraging technology for innovative solutions.
